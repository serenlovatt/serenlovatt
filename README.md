# 💫 About Me:
I'm currently working on<br><br>A regime-switching volatility forecaster for the S&P 500 and FTSE 100 using Hidden Markov Models to classify bull, volatile, and bear market states and generate regime-conditioned forecasts, benchmarked against GARCH and LSTM strategies.<br><br>I'm looking to collaborate on<br><br>ML applied to financial time series, structural break detection, volatility modelling, or anything at the intersection of statistical learning and quantitative finance. Also open to applied ML research projects in complex systems.<br><br>I'm looking for help with<br><br>Scaling HMM-based models to higher-frequency data and integrating more sophisticated emission distributions, if you've worked with MS-GARCH or Bayesian regime-switching approaches, I'd love to connect.<br><br>I'm currently learning<br><br>Deep learning architectures for sequential data (LSTMs, Transformers for time series), Bayesian inference methods, and advanced model evaluation techniques through my MSc in Complex Systems & Mathematics.<br>
